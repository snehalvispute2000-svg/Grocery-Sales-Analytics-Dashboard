🛒 **Grocery Sales Analytics Dashboard**
📌 **Project Overview**

The Grocery Sales Analytics Dashboard is an interactive Power BI dashboard developed to analyze grocery sales performance and provide actionable business insights. The dashboard enables users to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), evaluate outlet performance, analyze product categories, and explore sales trends through interactive visualizations.

This project was created as part of a Data Analyst Internship – Task 2: Data Visualization & Storytelling, with the objective of transforming raw sales data into meaningful business insights.

🎯 **Objectives**
Analyze overall grocery sales performance.
Monitor key business KPIs.
Identify high-performing product categories.
Compare outlet performance by location, size, and type.
Build an interactive dashboard that supports data-driven decision-making.
Present business insights using effective visual storytelling.

🧹 **Data Cleaning (Power Query)**

The dataset was cleaned and transformed in Power Query Editor before building the dashboard. The following preprocessing steps were performed:
Checked and handled missing values.
Corrected inconsistent data types.
Renamed columns for better readability.
Removed unnecessary columns (where applicable).
Verified duplicate records.
Applied appropriate formatting to numerical and categorical fields.
Ensured data consistency for analysis and reporting.

📈 **Key Business Insights**
Supermarket Type 1 contributes the highest overall sales.
Tier 3 outlets generate the highest sales among all outlet locations.
Fruits & Vegetables and Snack Foods are the top-selling product categories.
Medium-sized outlets contribute the largest share of total sales.
Average customer rating remains close to 4, indicating good customer satisfaction.
Regular Fat products generate higher sales compared to Low Fat products.
